How Booking Works

Book what's available now, or request a quote for future crops.

Book what's available now, or request a quote to confirm availability on anything else.

STEP 01

Book or request a quote

Find your varieties in the catalog. Every item shows whether it's available now or the soonest week we can have it ready. Book available stock, or request a quote to confirm availability and ship weeks on anything else — add quantities and submit.

STEP 02

Through your broker

Select your broker when you submit — Ball Seed, EHR, Express Seed, JVK, or McHutchison — and the order routes through them. Your relationship and your terms stay exactly as they are. Direct ordering is available for customers without a broker.

STEP 03

We confirm

We confirm pricing, quantities, and ship weeks. Nothing is final until you approve the returned quote.
